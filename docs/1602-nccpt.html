Bench Press Technique Seminar Part Three
 
 

Tom DeLong, Director of Education, will post a new Dartfish video on the basics of the bench press movement.

These videos address the mechanics of the powerlifting bench press as compared to the fitness/bodybuilding industry. Most in the training industry are very unfamiliar with applying biomechanical principles to this movement. Please note that the technique used for powerlifting is for performance enhancement in competition; however, the basic movement patterns are very similar and can be used for the fitness public, if applied correctly.
 
 
 
 
The Importance of Continuing Education
 
 

The fitness industry is constantly changing and evolving. As such, continuing education is required for fitness professionals to stay current with the latest guidelines and skills required to keep their clients safe while implementing innovative exercise techniques. Overall, continuing education is considered a way for fitness professionals to keep abreast of fitness industry trends and training strategies so they don’t lag behind or provide their clients with antiquated training or nutrition strategies.
