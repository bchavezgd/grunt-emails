7 Tips for a Better Bench Press
 
 

We must first ask ourselves what the goal of the movement is? This question will dictate whether we have a “fitness” set up or “competition” set up for the bench press. Your goal or movement dictates the movement as references by the specificity of training. For either fitness, bodybuilding, or performance, each person will have 7-points of contact: two feet, head, two scapula and two glutes. These contact points will ensure that you are creating a stable environment, which will help prevent injury and increase the chances of a successful lift.
